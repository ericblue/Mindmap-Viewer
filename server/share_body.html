SHARE YOUR MAPS

Luckily, sharing your maps is pretty simple! The MindMap Viewer application (http://eric-blue.com/projects/mindmapviewer/display.cgi) only needs 2 parameters in the URL:

  1. mmap_url = The publicly accessible URL of your mindmap (e.g. http://sample.org/mymap.mmap)
  2. format = The format of the map viwer: [flash  |  java]

INPUT
